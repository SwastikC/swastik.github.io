Observational Experience

Telescope Time at VLT/SPHERE

Principal investigator awarded 4.5 hours to observe three faint disks in ESO’s P116 cycle, with additional proposals submitted for upcoming cycles.

Co‑PI for VLT/SPHERE Proposals

Co‑investigator on three successful telescope time proposals at VLT/SPHERE, securing observation time for exoplanet studies.

Indian Telescope Proposals

Authored multiple observing proposals for 2 m and 3.6 m class telescopes in India, obtaining over 100 hours of observing time on the Hanle Faint Object Spectrograph Camera.

HIP41378f Campaign

Led a photometric and spectroscopic campaign on the exoplanet system HIP41378f, coordinating observations from Hanle and Kavalur as part of the GROWTH network.
