About Me

Swastik Chowbay is an astrophysicist and postdoctoral fellow at the Università degli Studi di Milano, Italy. He previously served as a postdoctoral researcher at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics in Bengaluru and earned his Ph.D in astrophysics from the same institute. With a Master of Science in Physics from the University of Hyderabad and a Bachelor of Science in Physics from the University of Calcutta, his academic journey reflects a deep commitment to understanding the universe. Proficient in multiple programming languages and data analysis tools, Swastik specializes in exoplanetary science and stellar astrophysics.
