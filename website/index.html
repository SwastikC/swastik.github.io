Research Highlights

Host‑Star Metallicity of Directly Imaged Wide‑Orbit Planets

Investigating how the chemical composition of stars influences the formation of giant planets, published in the Astronomical Journal (2021).

Galactic Chemical Evolution of Exoplanet Host‑Stars: Are High‑Mass Planetary Systems Young?

Examining the age and mass distribution of planetary systems across our galaxy to understand whether massive systems are young, published in the Astronomical Journal (2022).

Age Distribution of Exoplanet Host‑Stars

Combining chemical and kinematic proxies from GAIA DR3 to unravel the ages of stars hosting exoplanets, published in the Astronomical Journal (2023).
